# Pure Decoration

serving only an aesthetic purpose, providing no information, and having no functionality

   
   NoteText is only purely decorative if the words can be rearranged or substituted without
      changing their purpose.
   

   
   ExampleThe cover page of a dictionary has random words in very light text in the background.
