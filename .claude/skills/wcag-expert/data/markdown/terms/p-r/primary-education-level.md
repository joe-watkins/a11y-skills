# Primary Education Level

six year time period that begins between the ages of five and seven, possibly without
      any previous education
   

   
   NoteThis definition is based on the International Standard Classification of Education
      [[UNESCO](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#bib-unesco)].
