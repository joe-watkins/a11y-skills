# Lower Secondary Education Level

the two or three year period of education that begins after completion of six years
      of school and ends nine years after the beginning of [primary education](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#dfn-primary-education)

   
   NoteThis definition is based on the International Standard Classification of Education
      [[UNESCO](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#bib-unesco)].
