# Supplemental Content

additional [content](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#dfn-content) that illustrates or clarifies the primary content
   

   
   Example 1An audio version of a [web page](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#dfn-web-page-s).
   

   
   Example 2An illustration of a complex [process](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#dfn-processes).
   

   
   Example 3A paragraph summarizing the major outcomes and recommendations made in a research
      study.
