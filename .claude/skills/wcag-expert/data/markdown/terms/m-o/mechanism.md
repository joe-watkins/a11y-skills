# Mechanism

[process](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#dfn-processes) or technique for achieving a result
   

   
   Note 1The mechanism may be explicitly provided in the content, or may be [relied upon](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#dfn-relied-upon) to be provided by either the platform or by [user agents](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#dfn-user-agents), including [assistive technologies](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#dfn-assistive-technologies).
   

   
   Note 2The mechanism needs to meet all success criteria for the conformance level claimed.
