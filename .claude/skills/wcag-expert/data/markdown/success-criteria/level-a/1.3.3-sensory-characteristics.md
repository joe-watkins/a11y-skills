# 1.3.3 Sensory Characteristics (Level A)

**Guideline**: 1.3 Adaptable  
**Principle**: 1 Perceivable

## Understanding

Instructions provided for understanding and operating content do not rely solely on sensory characteristics of components such as shape, color, size, visual location, orientation, or sound.

## Full Description

Instructions provided for understanding and operating content do not rely solely on
      sensory characteristics of components such as shape, color, size, visual location, orientation,
      or sound.
   

  
  NoteFor requirements related to color, refer to [Guideline 1.4](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#distinguishable).

### Sufficient Techniques

### Common Failures

- **F14**: Failure of Success Criterion 1.3.3 due to identifying content only by its shape or location
- **F26**: Failure of Success Criterion 1.3.3 due to using a graphical symbol alone to convey information
