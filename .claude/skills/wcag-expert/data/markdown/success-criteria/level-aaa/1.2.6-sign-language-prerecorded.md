# 1.2.6 Sign Language (Prerecorded) (Level AAA)

**Guideline**: 1.2 Time-based Media  
**Principle**: 1 Perceivable

## Understanding

Sign language interpretation is provided for all prerecorded audio content in synchronized media.

## Full Description

[Sign language interpretation](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#dfn-sign-language-interpretation) is provided for all [prerecorded](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#dfn-prerecorded)
      [audio](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#dfn-audio) content in [synchronized media](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#dfn-synchronized-media).

## Techniques

### Sufficient Techniques

**Including a sign language interpreter in the video stream**

**Providing a synchronized video of the sign language interpreter that can be displayed in a different viewport or overlaid on the image by the player**
